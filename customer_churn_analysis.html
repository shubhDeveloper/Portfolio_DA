In our Bank Customer Churn Analysis report, we explore customer behaviors and churn factors. We start with an overview of our customer base by country, age, credit card status, and gender. This helps us spot patterns. We also look at churn rates, breaking them down by age, credit score, and account balance. This detailed analysis helps us create personalized strategies to keep more customers happy and loyal.
