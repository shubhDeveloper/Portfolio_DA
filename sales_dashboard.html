This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales and revenue, enabling data-driven decision-making. It features key performance indicators (KPIs) that display total revenue, total sales, and quantity sold, offering a quick overview of business performance. To facilitate in-depth analysis, I have incorporated dynamic filters for year and month, allowing users to drill down and analyze trends over specific periods.

The dashboard includes a bar chart to visualize revenue and sales by market, helping to compare performance across different regions. A line chart represents the revenue trend over time, making it easy to identify growth patterns, seasonal variations, and fluctuations in sales. Additionally, vertical bar charts highlight the top 5 customers and top 5 products, showcasing the most valuable contributors to business growth.

With its intuitive design and interactive elements, this dashboard empowers users to gain actionable insights, monitor key metrics, and make informed strategic decisions.
