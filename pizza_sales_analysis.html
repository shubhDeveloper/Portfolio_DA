In this Pizza Sales Analysis, we've delved into the heart of our pizza business to gain valuable insights. Our analysis covers a wide spectrum of essential metrics, starting with a comprehensive view of our total sales and average daily orders. We've also examined the average number of pizzas per order, offering a glimpse into customer preferences. Additionally, we've tracked sales trends over months, providing a clear visualization of our performance. To cater to customer preferences, we've analyzed sales by preferred category and size. Finally, we've created bar charts pinpointing the busiest days and peak hours, enabling us to optimize staffing and resources for a seamless customer experience.
